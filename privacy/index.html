Privacy

Your Portfolio, Your Device

MyGoldFolio is designed around local-first data ownership. No account required.

What Is Never Collected

  • Holdings and portfolio values
  • Purchase amounts and sell records
  • Personal profile or account identity

What Is Optional

  • Anonymous usage analytics
  • Enabled only with explicit consent
  • Can be turned off anytime in Settings

On-Device Storage

Portfolio data is stored locally on your device. MyGoldFolio does not upload holdings or financial records to SmartDailyApps servers.

Exports & Backups

Backup and export files are created only when you request them. Data leaves your device only when you explicitly share or save those files externally.

Price Data Requests

To show live gold prices, the app queries third-party price APIs. These requests are for market data retrieval and do not include your holdings details.

Consent Control

Analytics consent is explicit and reversible. You can withdraw consent at any time from within the app settings.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact info@mygoldfolio.de.
