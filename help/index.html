Setup & Portfolio

How do I add my first holding? ▾

Go to Portfolio, tap the add button, and enter category, weight, cost, and purchase date. Save to start live valuation and P&L tracking immediately.

Is MyGoldFolio free? ▾

Yes. Free core tracking is available with no ads and no subscription. Premium is an optional one-time Lifetime unlock for higher limits and advanced tools.

What does Premium add? ▾

Premium focuses on larger portfolios and deeper analysis: higher limits, extended chart/history access, richer export workflow, and advanced decision-support modules.

Prices & Charts

Which chart windows are available? ▾

Price chart windows include 7D, 30D, 60D, 120D, and ALL. Premium unlocks full-range chart access and deeper history depth.

How is price history stored? ▾

History retention is plan-aware. Free keeps a shorter rolling history, while Premium stores more points for long-range context and export workflows.

Why does the app use multiple price sources? ▾

MyGoldFolio uses fallback logic to improve reliability. If one source is temporarily unavailable, the app can continue with another source.

Privacy & Data Control

Where is my data stored? ▾

Portfolio and holding data stays on your device. Optional anonymous analytics is off by default and only runs if you explicitly opt in.

How do backup and restore work? ▾

In Settings, open Data & Export to create or import backup files. Data leaves the device only when you explicitly share exported files.

Can I disable analytics later? ▾

Yes. You can switch it off anytime in Settings. The app remains fully usable without analytics enabled.
